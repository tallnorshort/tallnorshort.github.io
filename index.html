Top four American Comedic Actors

Adam Sandler

Started his career in SNL, rose as one of Hollywood's top go to guys with such hits as; Waterboy, 50 first dates, and The longest Yard. Adam Sandler is still producing hits to this day, recently releasing a boxoffice hit called 'Hidden Gems'. But is Adam Sandler too played out? But Is he America's number oone movie comedic?

Jim Carrey

An American Icon, there not much you can't say about Jim Carrey. His hits have spanned across three decades, with such classics as 'The Mask', 'Bruce Almighty', and 'Ace Ventura: Pet Detective'. But can he rank amoung our fellow contestants? Jim Carrey has taken some flops with such movies as 'Number 23". Is he America's favorite movie comic?

Will Ferrell

Will Ferrell, a name that goes in sync with San Diego, CA, is an American actor with many hits under his belt(not to mention catch phrases). With classics such as Anchorman('I am in a glassbox of emotions') and Stepbrothers('Don't Touch my Drumset!), Will Ferrell is one comic who is a constant draw, but has failed to capture the same size audiences he once did in the pass. Is he America's favorite movie comic?

Ben Stiller

Ben Stiller is a comic powerhouse, with his first big break being 'Something about Mary'. Since then he has given such preformances as 'Tropic Thunder'(dual threat as director), 'Meet the Parents', and 'Night at the Museum'. He is not to be underestimated in his comic ability and is still one of America's favorite leading men but is hot and cold when it comes to boxoffice preformances. Is he America's Best Movie Comic?
